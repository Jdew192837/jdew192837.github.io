Bitcoin

Released in 2009, Bitcoin is the world's highest priced cryptocurrency and was the first decentralized digital money. In addition Bitcoin is used worldwide and does not require a central bank or administration, but instead uses a system of cryptography called blockchain. Bitcoin was created as a reward for mining.

Name: All Bitcoin $5000+

Ethereum

Created in 2014, Ethereum is an open-source blockchain-based computing platform, meaning the community can have input and access to the code that has smart functionality. It can support a version of Nakamoto through transaction based state transitions. In 2016, Ethereum was split into two ETC and ET.

Name: All Ethereum $600+

Ripple

Ripple is a cryptocurrency with a real-time gross settlement system, currency exchange and remittance system devloped by the Ripple Company. Ripple was released in 2012 and can also run without the Ripple Company via internet providers or other organizations such as the Massachusetts Institute of Technology.

Name: All Ripple Price < 1$

Litecoin

Litecoin also known as LTC is a cryptocurrency that is opensource and under the MIT/X11 license. The creation and transfer of this coin is also cryptographic and does not have a central authority. Litecoin's idea was based on the renowned BTC. It has technical and similar aspects to BTC as well.

Name: All Litecoin $100+
